How the colors are defined

Each thumbnail is a weight map. Bright orange in Layer 1 and bright cyan in Layer 2 indicate strong positive weights. Cool blue indicates strong negative weights. Very dark regions are close to zero, meaning that pixel contributes only weakly to that learned feature.
